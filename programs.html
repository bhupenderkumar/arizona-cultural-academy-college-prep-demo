Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is designed for children aged 3-4, focusing on social-emotional development, literacy, and numeracy skills. Our experienced teachers use play-based learning to create a nurturing environment that encourages curiosity and creativity.

🎒

Elementary

In our Elementary program, students in grades K-5 engage in a rigorous curriculum that includes reading, writing, mathematics, science, and social studies. Our small class sizes allow for personalized attention and support to ensure every student thrives.

🎒

Middle/Upper

In our Middle and Upper School program, students in grades 6-12 explore advanced courses in humanities, arts, and sciences. Our faculty mentors offer guidance and support to help students navigate academic and personal challenges.

⚖️

STEM

Our STEM program is designed to foster critical thinking, problem-solving, and innovation skills. Students engage in hands-on activities, projects, and competitions that prepare them for careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

🎭

Arts

At Arizona Cultural Academy & College Prep, we believe that arts education is essential to a well-rounded education. Our students engage in visual arts, music, drama, and dance programs that foster creativity, self-expression, and appreciation for diverse cultures.

🏋️‍♀️

Athletics

Our Athletics program offers a range of sports teams and activities that promote physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship. Students can choose from soccer, basketball, volleyball, and other sports, competing against local schools and developing their skills in a supportive environment.
